Features

#1 - Timetable

Construct your timetable depending on your personal needs. Easily set up your timetable with just a few simple steps on our Dashboard.

Timetables are the perfect to solution the schedule your classes.
You can set a classes' subject, channel, start, and end time.
It is also possible to start an automated check once the class starts. Of course, also the time the check will take, the notifications are customizable.

#2 - Calculation

Feel the need to quickly solve some math problems?
Just use our new calculation feature!

#3 - Attendance

Easier than ever before to check the attendance of your students or friends. This feature also comes with customizable time and roles to check.

Your can check your friends or students attendence by using this feature.
Students have to react to a message sent by our bot within a certain time to be marked as present.

#4 - Alert

You are a teacher, and want to send a message to all your students?
This is now easily possible through the use of our alert feature.
